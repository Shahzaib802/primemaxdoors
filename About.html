About Us


At PrimeMax Fiber Doors, we pride ourselves on being the leading manufacturers of premium quality fiber doors in Pakistan. Our collection of fiber doors is designed to meet the highest standards of elegance, functionality, and durability. We offer a diverse range of ultra-modern, classic, and sophisticated designs that are perfect for any home or commercial property. From residential spaces like lounges, kitchens, and bathrooms to offices and commercial properties, our custom fiber doors are tailored to suit your specific needs. With an extensive selection of over fifty high-quality PVC fiber doors, we ensure that every product we offer is both affordable and of the highest standard. At PrimeMax, our mission is to deliver superior fiber doors at market-competitive prices without compromising on quality. We are committed to providing top-notch poly doors across Pakistan, ensuring that every customer enjoys exceptional performance, style, and value with every installation.
